F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an online examination system?

An online examination system is a digital platform designed to create, administer, and evaluate exams online. It allows institutions to conduct exams remotely with features like automated grading, AI proctoring, and real-time monitoring.

How secure is an online examination system?

Online examination systems are built with robust security features, including end-to-end encryption, AI-based proctoring (face and voice recognition, behavior tracking), and multi-factor authentication to prevent cheating and unauthorized access.

How does AI proctoring work in online exams?

AI proctoring uses advanced algorithms to monitor student behavior during the exam. It detects suspicious activities like unusual eye movements, background noise, and unauthorized objects in the room, helping to prevent cheating without the need for human proctors.

Can online exams accommodate both objective and subjective questions?

Yes, most online exam systems support both objective (MCQs, true/false) and subjective (essay, long-form answers) question types, making them suitable for a wide range of assessments, including academic, corporate, and psychometric exams.

What are the benefits of using an online examination system?

The benefits include greater flexibility, scalability, reduced administrative burden, faster result processing, enhanced security with AI proctoring, and the ability to conduct exams from any location, ensuring accessibility for a larger number of candidates.

How do I use an online exam system?

1. Register or Sign In: Create an account on the online exam platform or sign in with existing credentials.
2. Set Up the Exam: For admins, configure the exam by choosing the question type (objective/subjective), setting time limits, and uploading questions.
3. Schedule the Exam: Select the date and time for the exam, and assign it to the relevant student batch.
4. Student Authentication: Ensure students go through the authentication process (e.g., two-factor authentication or ID verification).
5. Conduct the Exam: Monitor the exam using AI proctoring tools or live proctors to maintain exam integrity.
6. Review Results: After the exam, the system will automatically generate results, which admins can review and students can access.
